wildlife

Located in the heart of Nairobi, Nairobi National Park is one of the only national parks on earth that borders a capital city. If you do not have much time to go to other national parks, this is worth a visit. You get to see most of the animals that you would on a regular safari. One can see many animals and get some excellent photos. Arriving early at about 6:00am is recommended.
