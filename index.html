The Drive in the National Park

Located in the heart of Nairobi, Nairobi National Park is one of the only national parks on earth that borders a capital city. If you do not have much time to go to other national parks, this is worth a visit. You get to see most of the animals that you would on a regular safari. It is important to hire a driver who is familiar with the game park, routes and sites as he or she would know where the animals are. One can see many animals and get some excellent photos. Arriving early at about 6:00am is recommended. You will get to see the sunrise. There is also a higher probability of seeing many more animals. Animals reported to have been seen include, monkeys, giraffes, hippos, buffalo, rhinos, zebra's, antelopes, and many other animals including lions if you are lucky.
